Prevent getting sick from COVID-19

Home

  • Wear Your Mask over your nose and mouth and secure it under your chin.
  • Wash your hands
  • Stay at least 6 Feet Apart and Avoid Crowds

Travel

  • The safest thing to do is to stay home
  • Do not travel if you test positive; immediately isolate yourself, and follow public health recommendations.
  • Consider getting tested with a viral test 1-3 days before you travel. Keep a copy of your test results with you during travel; you may be asked for them.
  • You may have been exposed to COVID-19 on your travels. You may feel well and not have any symptoms, but you can still spread the virus to others.
  • Consider isolate yourself up to 10 days, if possible.

Healthy Way to cope with Stress

  • Take breaks from watching, reading, or listening to news stories, including those on social media. Hearing about the pandemic repeatedly can be upsetting.
  • Know what to do if you are sick and are concerned about COVID-19. Contact a health professional before you start any self-treatment for COVID-19.
  • Take care of your body: Get plenty of sleep; try to eat healthy, well-balanced meals; exercise regularly.
  • Make time to unwind. Try to do some other activities you enjoy.
  • Connect with others. Talk with people you trust about your concerns and how you are feeling.
